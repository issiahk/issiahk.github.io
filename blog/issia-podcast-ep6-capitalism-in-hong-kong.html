ISSIA Podcast EP6: Capitalism in Hong Kong

By ISSIA HK
Published on June 4th, 2020

Inspired by Jessica's article, Percy (Host), Justin, Tungsten and Calvary (guest), along with Jessica (author) discuss capitalism in Hong Kong. Discussed topic include: the relationship between capitalism and the state, the nature of workplace democracy (syndicalism) within the free market and possible alternatives to the "profit motive".
