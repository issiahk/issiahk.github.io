ISSIA HK Leadership Positions Now Open!

Administrative, operations, events-based roles and more all open for application!

ISSIA (Inter-school Social Issues Association) is an international, student-led group based in Hong Kong. We aim to foster stronger inter-school links and create a network for the improvement of social conditions in Hong Kong and beyond. Currently, ISSIA has over 130+ active contributors and designers from 28+ schools throughout Hong Kong. In addition, we have also expanded internationally, with contributors from the U.S, India, Philippines, Russia, Kazakhstan, Vietnam and the U.K!

We produce a variety of multimedia content, including magazines, podcasts, webinars, surveys and videos. In the forthcoming year, we intend to launch a series of live events and programmes, ranging from workshops, conferences and the introduction of school chapters.

ISSIA prides itself on having an active community and network of passionate individuals, all working to shed light on pressing issues and providing an inclusive platform for social engagement and student voice; the notion of civil discourse has never been more important and is central to our driving ethos - one we will continue to uphold. Please visit our website at issiahk.org

This form is for individuals that are interested in administrative/operational leadership openings in ISSIA. By taking on these roles, you will procure solid managerial/leadership experience and will be closely involved with the day-to-day running of ISSIA! These roles form the backbone of ISSIA, in addition to covering ambitious projects outside of the ISSIA Magazine (multimedia projects, physical events, outreach and development etc.). If you are interested in editorial/design positions, please apply here: https://forms.gle/KhMJjTmJzsQMHCrD9

Deadline: 13th July 2020
